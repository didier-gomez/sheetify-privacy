# Privacy Policy for Sheetify

Last updated: 2026-06-08

Sheetify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All image 
processing occurs entirely on your device.

**Advertising:** The free tier displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may 
collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy: 
https://policies.google.com/privacy

**In-app purchases:** Purchases are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not 
receive or store payment information.

**Permissions:** The app requests storage/media access solely to read the image 
you select. No data leaves your device.
